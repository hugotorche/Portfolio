Key points:
  • You do a Bullshit Job when it is useless and stupid, even in your own eyes, but you need it to live.

  • A Bullshit Job is often well paid and socially respected, but it could be done away with without anyone noticing its absence. A Bullshit Job is often anglicized/abstract and its existence does not depend on rational economic reasons.

  • The Bullshit Job is often created to flatter one's ego, imitate the competition, fix absurd mistakes or create false problems.

  • There are different types of Bullshit Jobs: stooges, flunkies, tinkerers, tickers, "little bosses", etc.

  • Bullshit Jobs are not without consequences (bore-out, brown-out) and cause many reconversions.

  • It continues to exist, because it is more difficult to eliminate existing jobs than to create new ones, which are also useless.

  • Their presence reveals the existence of a feudal system of managerial type.

  • Universal income is one of the only solutions to remedy this increasingly problematic situation.

  • It is essential to keep a work/life balance, be attentive to the signs of a Bullshit Job and stay active.
