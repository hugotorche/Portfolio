Key points:
  • Our emotions have a constant impact on our lives and decisions. With work, it is possible to master them.

  • Emotional intelligence is based on self-awareness, mindfulness, and the ability to engage in constructive behaviors.

  • Self-awareness requires listening to one's emotions to understand their origin and manifestations.

  • Considering long-term interests and paying daily attention to emotions and relationships. Adopt good habits of life and thought hygiene.

  • Managing emotions well means always taking the time to reflect. One must be aware of the fluctuation of emotions in decision making.

  • Social awareness involves observing and listening to others. Do not hesitate to ask questions, show interest.

  • Balanced relationships require building trusting relationships and communicating in an open and respectful manner.

  • The idea of emotional intelligence is advancing in society today, with differences among social groups.
