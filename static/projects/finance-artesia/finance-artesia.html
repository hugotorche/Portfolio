The Case Study Artesia is divided in three parts:
  • A Financial Valuation with Intrinsic Approach of the company based on Cost of financing and Free Cash-Flow method.

  • A Financial Valuation with Relative Approach of the company based Multiple approach: Sales, EBIT, EBITDA and Net Income Multiples.

  • A Recommendations and explanations section.
Results:
  • Intrinsic Approach provided a value of 223 Million Euros while Relative Approach provided a value of 183 Million Euros.

  • A recommendation would be to include an Earn-out term in the acquisition for the difference amount between the two approaches.
