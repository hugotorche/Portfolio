The Case Study Auchan is divided in two parts:
  • A Financial Valuation of the company Le Gaulois based on historical and forecasted data. For this section, Free Cash-Flow and Goodwill methods were applied.

  • A Financial Analysis of the company Auchan based on the final valuation method of Le Gaulois and the repayment capacity of the group. The final valuation method chosen was EBE Multiple.
Results:
  • From Financial Valuation, the company Le Gaulois was valued at around 1,2 Million Euros.

  • Financial Analysis showed that, despite a new price of 1,7 Million Euros for this acquisition, Auchan had the repayment capacity required.
