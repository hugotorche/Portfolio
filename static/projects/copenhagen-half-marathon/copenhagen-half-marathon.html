I have been selected to go on exchange to Copenhagen Business School next semester. What better way to accompany this experience than with a sporting challenge! For the year 2021 I had set myself the goal of running a half-marathon in 2021. Initially, I wanted to achieve this goal in summer but when I saw that the Copenhagen Half Marathon was organized on September 19th, I jumped on the opportunity. The goals of the project are:

  • Sporting: Finish the race and reach a time of less than 2 hours
  • International: Discover the Nordic culture and meet international people
  • Environmental & Social: Participate in a solidarity event
  • Communication: Promote the school's image by informing the stakeholders
  • Personal: Commitment, Autonomy and Agility
