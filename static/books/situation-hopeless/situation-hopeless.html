Key points:
  • You are indeed the architect of your own unhappiness, and therefore by association of ideas, of your own happiness. The solution lies in your hands.

  • The notion of unhappiness is unique to each person.

  • The notion of unhappiness is particularly fertile, and only you have the power to cultivate it.

  • You will find as many reasons to be unhappy in your relationships with others as you do within yourself.

  • It is as easy to mourn in your own unhappiness as it is to create an environment that is prompt to happiness and personal enrichment.

  • If unhappiness is at hand, only you hold the keys to your personal and individual fulfillment.
