Key points:
  • It is necessary to doubt because it allows one to question all these certainties to search for a more enlightened truth and thus to be free.

  • To know oneself and the world better one must combine one's senses with one's understanding.

  • The other allows you to question your world and thus become free.

  • Free will is the key: will and understanding. Be careful not to rely solely on one's will for lack of knowledge.

  • A free man is not in ignorance but builds the world.
